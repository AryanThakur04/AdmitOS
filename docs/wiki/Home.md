## Product Overview

AdmitOS (Autonomous Education Operations Engine) is a full-stack admission CRM with:

1. **Lead Intake** — CSV, Google Sheets, Facebook, Website, WhatsApp referrals
2. **AI Scoring** — Tier assignment + conversion probability + aging score
3. **Risk Detector** — Automated alerts for SLA breaches
4. **Counsellor Analytics** — Performance and lost-reason dashboards
5. **Document Engine** — Heatmaps + acceptance pack PDF generation
6. **WhatsApp Tools** — Follow-up message simulator
7. **Journey Replay** — Touchpoint timeline per student
8. **Referral Tracking** — Multi-level referral chains
9. **Alert Center** — Unified risk notifications
